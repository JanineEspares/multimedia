Frame-by-frame precision work with professional VFX compositing

Project Overview

This project showcases a 1-minute rotoscoped video created using industry-standard tools including CapCut, Adobe After Effects, and Adobe Premiere Pro. The work demonstrates meticulous attention to detail with frame-by-frame precision, professional VFX compositing, and advanced motion tracking techniques.

Duration 1 Minute Rotoscope
Frames 1,800
Tools 3 Major

Step-by-Step Tutorial

01

Select Reference Material

Choose and review reference material to understand the techniques and methods you'll apply in your rotoscope project. Study the movements, transitions, and visual effects carefully before beginning your work.

View on YouTube
02

Cut into 5 Minutes

Use CapCut to condense your footage into a 5-minute video. This editing software makes it easy to trim, cut, and arrange your clips. Focus on selecting the best moments and maintaining smooth transitions between scenes.

03

Import to After Effects

Open clips in Adobe After Effects for rotoscoping.

04

Create Roto Layer

Add a new layer and prepare it for masking.

05

Use Roto Brush Tool

Select your subject using the Object/Rotobrush tool.

06

Apply Color Fill

Add a color fill behind the rotoscoped subject.

07

Repeat for All Clips

Apply steps to each segment until clip 13 is completed.

08

Render Roto Clips

Export all finished rotoscoped clips.

09

Import to Premiere Pro

Bring clips into Adobe Premiere Pro.

10

Add Background Colors

Place colors behind your subject for visual style.

11

Export Final Video

Export from Premiere Pro the edited sequence with background color.

12

Add Effects & Lyrics

Use CapCut to add text, effects, and finishing touches. Then export.
